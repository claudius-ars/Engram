## Raw Concept

A follow-up inspection of Well A-14 was conducted 30 days after the March 2024 pressure test. All monitored parameters remained nominal — surface casing pressure steady at 0 psi, production casing pressure within operating envelope, and no anomalous temperature readings on the distributed temperature sensing fiber. The production tubing hanger seal test confirmed barrier integrity with a pressure hold of 2,500 psi for 15 minutes and zero bleed-off. Corrosion coupon retrieval showed metal loss rates within acceptable limits at 2.1 mils per year. The well was returned to full production rate following inspection completion.
