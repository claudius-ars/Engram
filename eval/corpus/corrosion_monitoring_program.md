## Raw Concept

The corrosion monitoring program uses a multi-layer approach combining cathodic protection, chemical inhibition, and physical inspection. Cathodic protection systems on subsea infrastructure are surveyed annually using ROV-mounted CP probes, targeting a potential of -850mV to -1050mV vs Ag/AgCl. Corrosion inhibitor is continuously injected at the wellhead at a rate of 50 ppm based on produced water volume, with batch treatments quarterly. Wall thickness measurements are taken at 24 critical monitoring points using ultrasonic testing on a 6-month cycle, with intelligent pigging of the export pipeline annually. Corrosion coupons are retrieved and analyzed every 90 days, with acceptable metal loss rates below 5 mils per year for carbon steel and 1 mil per year for CRA-clad surfaces.
