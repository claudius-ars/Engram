## Raw Concept

A controlled annular pressure bleed-off was performed on Well B-7 on April 2, 2024 to reduce the A-annulus sustained casing pressure from 450 psi to 120 psi. The procedure followed the SCP diagnostic protocol: the annulus was bled down through the kill line at a controlled rate of 50 psi per minute, with continuous monitoring of the B-annulus and surface casing vent pressure for any crossflow indication. The bleed-off yielded 2.3 barrels of gas-cut fluid, confirming the micro-annulus communication pathway. Pressure rebuilt to 280 psi within 6 hours after the bleed-off, consistent with the slow migration rate profile. The next scheduled bleed-off is set for May 2024 per the monitoring plan.
