## Raw Concept

Well A-14 underwent a scheduled casing integrity pressure test on March 15, 2024 as part of the annual barrier verification program. The 9-5/8" production casing was tested to 3,200 psi for 30 minutes with zero observed pressure decline, resulting in a PASS classification per API RP 90-2. The test was performed with the well shut-in and the Christmas tree isolated. Pre-test and post-test caliper logs confirmed no measurable casing wear or deformation. The previous test (March 2023) had also passed at the same test pressure. Results have been recorded in the well integrity register and the barrier status updated to "verified."
