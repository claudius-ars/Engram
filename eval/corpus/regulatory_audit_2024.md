## Raw Concept

The annual regulatory compliance audit was conducted March 18-20, 2024 by the offshore safety authority. The audit covered well integrity management, environmental compliance, and emergency preparedness. Two minor findings were issued: (1) three wells had barrier verification documentation that exceeded the 30-day update requirement by 5-12 days, and (2) the corrosion inhibitor injection rate on the C-3 flowline was 15% below the prescribed minimum for two weeks in January. No major findings or stop-work orders were issued. Corrective action plans are due by end of Q3 2024, with a follow-up verification audit scheduled for October.
