## Raw Concept

The Q2 2024 production forecast targets 4,200 barrels of oil per day (BOPD) from three active producing wells: A-14 (1,800 BOPD), B-7 (1,200 BOPD at reduced rate due to sustained casing pressure), and C-3 (1,200 BOPD). The forecast assumes no unplanned downtime and a planned 5-day maintenance window in May for C-3 turnaround. Natural decline rates of 8-12% annually have been incorporated based on reservoir simulation updates from December 2023. Upside potential exists if the B-7 workover is advanced to Q2, which could restore full production capacity of 1,600 BOPD. Water cut is forecast to increase from 35% to 38% across the field during the quarter.
