## Raw Concept

The well integrity management system follows ISO 16530 Part 2 for the full well lifecycle, from design through abandonment. Primary and secondary well barriers must be independently verified at intervals not exceeding 12 months, with interim monitoring through annular pressure surveillance. All barrier element failures must be documented in the well integrity register within 24 hours. The policy mandates real-time pressure monitoring on all active wells, with automatic alerts triggered when sustained casing pressure exceeds 150 psi or when annular pressure builds exceed 10 psi per hour. Remediation decisions follow a risk matrix combining barrier redundancy level, hydrocarbon exposure category, and environmental sensitivity classification.
