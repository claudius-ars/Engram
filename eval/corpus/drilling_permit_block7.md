## Raw Concept

A temporary drilling permit was issued for Block 7 under an emergency variance provision on September 1, 2019. The variance allowed drilling of two appraisal wells (B7-X1 and B7-X2) using a modified casing program that reduced the intermediate casing setting depth by 200 feet to accommodate unexpected shallow gas hazards encountered during the B7-X1 spud. The permit required enhanced kick detection procedures and continuous gas monitoring during the variance period. The variance expired on December 31, 2019 and was not renewed due to the completion of both appraisal wells. All subsequent drilling in Block 7 must follow the standard permit requirements.
