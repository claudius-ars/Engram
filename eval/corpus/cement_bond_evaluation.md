## Raw Concept

Cement bond quality is evaluated using a combination of cement bond logs (CBL) and variable density logs (VDL) run after each cement job. The CBL amplitude threshold for acceptable bond is set at 4 millivolts or less in the 9-5/8" casing, corresponding to approximately 80% cement coverage. VDL signatures are classified as good bond (parallel chevron pattern), partial bond (intermittent chevron with casing arrivals), or free pipe (strong casing signal, no formation arrivals). When channeling is detected above a critical zone, remedial options include squeeze cementing, expandable liner patches, or mechanical isolation using bridge plugs. The evaluation protocol requires comparison with the original cement placement model to identify systematic issues in displacement efficiency.
