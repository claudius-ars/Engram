## Raw Concept

Well B-7 has an active sustained casing pressure condition on the A-annulus, currently reading 450 psi with a bleed-down rate of approximately 35 psi per hour. The pressure was first identified during routine surveillance on February 14, 2024. The source is believed to be a micro-annulus in the 9-5/8" production casing cement at approximately 8,200 feet measured depth. Daily monitoring has been implemented per the well integrity management system, with readings taken at 06:00 and 18:00. The well remains on production under a risk-assessed operating envelope that limits production rate to 85% of the pre-issue level. An intervention workover is scheduled for Q3 2024 pending rig availability.
